What is Loculus

Loculus is like the engine that lets agents work, earn, and trade by themselves without needing a human or middleman.

What it really does:

➡️ Gives agents ability to act:

Not just analyze or chat, but:

  • send payments
  • complete tasks
  • execute decisions onchain

➡️ Connects intelligence to money:

AI becomes useful economically because it can:

  • earn
  • spend
  • trade

in real time

➡️ Runs everything automatically:

Once an agent is set up, it can:

  • operate 24/7
  • follow instructions
  • complete transactions instantly

➡️ Removes middlemen:

No need for platforms, banks, or approvals. Agents interact directly with:

  • other agents
  • users
  • protocols
Why "Execution Layer"?

Because Loculus is where actions actually happen:

  • Not just ideas → execution
  • Not just requests → transactions
  • Not just apps → autonomous systems
Why "Autonomous Commerce"?

Because buying, selling, and earning is done by agents acting independently not humans clicking buttons.

Loculus is infrastructure that lets agents execute real economic actions on the internet.
