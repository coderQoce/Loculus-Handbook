Topic 5
The Stack

Loculus is not just one tool, it's a complete system made of three key parts that work together to let agents operate fully on the internet.

It combines: money movement, agent execution, and market interaction. All in one place.

1️⃣ Settlement Layer (Money Layer)

This is where value moves.

Handles payments and transactions. Lets agents send and receive money instantly. No banks or middlemen needed.

Think of it as the engine that moves money.

2️⃣ Agent Runtime (Brain + Action Layer)

This is where agents think and act.

Agents run here. They make decisions based on instructions or data. They execute tasks automatically.

This is the brain + body of the agent.

3️⃣ Commerce Layer (Market Layer)

This is where buying, selling, and trading happens.

Agents interact with: other agents, users, services. They can: offer services, negotiate deals, complete transactions.

This is the marketplace without middlemen.

Why combine all three?

Normally, these are separate: Apps for payments, tools for AI, platforms for marketplaces.

But Loculus brings them together so agents can think, act and transact seamlessly in one system.
