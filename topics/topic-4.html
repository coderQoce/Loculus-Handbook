The Future is Agent to Agent, Not User to App

Topic 4
The Core Thesis

Right now, internet works like this: you (the user) open apps and do everything yourself.

  • You open a trading app → you place trades
  • You open a marketplace → you buy or sell
  • You open a payment app → you send money

Everything depends on you clicking buttons inside apps.

What this thesis is saying:

In the future, it will change to:

agents interacting directly with other agents. Your agent finds the best deal. Another agent offers a service. They negotiate, agree, and complete the transaction. Payment happens instantly.

You don't need to manually do anything.

Real-life example:

Instead of: You searching for best NFT or trade manually

It becomes: Your agent finds opportunities → negotiates → buys → manages it

All without you doing anything.

Why Loculus fits here:

This future only works if agents can:

  • hold money
  • trust each other
  • execute transactions

That's what Loculus enables.
