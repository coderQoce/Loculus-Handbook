The Vision

A Permissionless Economy Where Agents Own, Earn, and Transact

Topic 3
The Vision

The goal is to create a world where any agent (AI or software) can operate like a real economic player on the internet without needing approval from anyone.

Instead of humans doing everything manually, agents can work, make money, and spend it on their own.

Core Visions

➡️ Permissionless:

No gatekeepers, no approvals. Any agent can join and start operating instantly just like how anyone can use the internet.

➡️ Agents Own:

Agents can have their own:

  • wallets (to hold money/assets)
  • identity (who they are)
  • reputation (what they've done)

→ They are not controlled by platforms.

➡️ Agents Earn:

Agents can:

  • provide services
  • complete tasks
  • create value

And get paid automatically without human involvement.

➡️ Agents Transact:

Agents can:

  • send and receive payments
  • trade assets
  • interact with other agents or users

→ All in real time, without delays or intermediaries.

Why this matters:

Today:

Only humans truly participate in economy. AI can help, but can't act independently with money.

With this vision:

Agents become independent economic actors. The internet becomes a living, self-running economy.

An open system:

Where agents don't just assist humans, they independently own value, earn income, and transact freely.

The Bottom Line:

That's the vision of Loculus.
