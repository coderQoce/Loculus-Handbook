Topic 8
Key Terms

➡️ Autonomous:

Something that works by itself without needing human control.

➡️ Autonomous Commerce:

Buying, selling, and earning done by agents automatically, not humans.

➡️ Agent Runtime:

The environment where agents run, think, and execute actions.

➡️ Programmable Money:

Money that can follow rules and send itself automatically based on conditions.

➡️ Commerce Layer:

Where trading, buying, and selling happens between agents or users.

➡️ Agent-to-Agent (A2A):

When agents interact and transact directly with each other without human involvement.

➡️ Digital Economy:

An economy where value is created, exchanged, and managed online.
