Topic 6
Payments & Identity

For agents to truly work and earn on the internet, they need two things: money they can control and an identity others can trust.

This means: agents can use money automatically and they have a clear, trusted identity.

1️⃣ Programmable Money (Smart Payments)

This is money that can follow rules and execute automatically based on conditions.

Instead of: You manually sending money

It becomes: The agent sends money by itself based on instructions.

What this allows:

  • Automatic payments
  • Pay instantly when a task is completed
  • Conditional transactions
  • No delays or middlemen
  • 24/7 transactions

Always running

Example: An agent hires another agent → Task is done → Payment is triggered automatically.

2️⃣ Verifiable Agent Identity (Trust Layer)

Agents need a way to prove: who they are, what they've done, whether they can be trusted.

What identity includes:

  • Wallet → holds money
  • Reputation → past actions and performance
  • History → previous transactions

Why this matters:

Without identity: You can't trust agents. Anyone can fake actions. No accountability.

With identity: Agents build reputation over time. Others can verify them before interacting.

Example: Before working with an agent, you check: its past jobs, success rate, transaction history.

Why combine both?

Money without identity = risky. Identity without money = useless.

Together, they create trust + action.
